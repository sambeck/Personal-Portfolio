POLYPHONIC

Polyphonic is a book on synthpop, a genre of music emerging out of the New Wave movement in the early seventies. Originally defined by its heavy usage of synthesizers, synthpop grew to encompass much more that that. Polyphonic celebrates not only the musicians who defined these decades but the art and technology that shaped them.
